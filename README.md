## 🛡️ Current Interest: Robust Watermarking, AI Provenance & Multimedia Forensics

I am currently exploring research directions related to trustworthy generative AI, including:

- **Robust Watermarking for AI-generated Visual Content**
- **Image-to-Video Watermark Robustness**
- **AI Provenance and Content Authenticity**
- **Multimedia Forensics and Security**
- **Traceability and Attribution of Generated Media**
- **Cross-modal Generative Transformations**
- **End-to-End AI Threat Models**
- **Watermark Recovery from Transformed Video Frames**
- **Tamper-resistant Visual AI Systems**
- **Deep Learning-based Copyright Protection**

The main research idea is to study how hidden watermarks can survive complex generative transformations such as image-to-video generation, diffusion-based editing, style transfer, compression, frame interpolation, geometric distortion, and other real-world post-processing attacks.
